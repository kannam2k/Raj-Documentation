### HOW TO USE OKTA TRAINING GUIDE

### Purpose

This guide helps new users understand how to use Okta, including the dashboard, adding new apps, accessing the user profile, adding a new section, and the benefits of using Okta.

### Sign in to Okta

On the Anisa International sign-in page, enter your **Username** and **Password**, then click the **Verify** button. You will be directed to the Anisa International dashboard page.

{% hint style="info" %}
_Note: Select the **Keep me signed in** checkbox to remember your username and password. Select **Forgot password** and follow the instructions to recover your password._
{% endhint %}

### Introduction to User Dashboard

The Okta End-User Dashboard is a platform where you can securely access your enterprise applications. From the dashboard, you can manage your Okta account, search your apps, read notifications, organize apps, or request apps.

On the left pane, you can see the **My Apps** section. On the main screen, you can see recently used apps, your favorites, and work-related sections. You can also customize the dashboard layout by adding sections and organizing apps in a way that meets your needs.

<div align="left"><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Unknown image (208)" alt=""></div>

### Adding Apps

You can add most standard online apps to your Okta End-User Dashboard with a single click. To do this, you must first sign in to your account to verify your identity.

Click **Add Apps**, search for your app, and click **Add**. You can also use the **Filter** drop-down to select a specific category.

_Note: For some applications, you might see a **Request** button instead of **Add**. Click **Request**, and your request is sent to your IT department. You will receive a notification on your apps dashboard when your request is approved. Newly assigned apps appear at the top of the app catalog on your apps dashboard._

### Searching Apps

You can quickly search for apps already added to the dashboard by using the **Search** field. The results will be displayed.

### Adding a Section

Click the **Add Section** button. The **Create Section** pop-up appears.

<div align="left"><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Unknown image (211)" alt="" width="375"></div>

Enter a name in the **Section name** field and click the **Save** button. The **Drag apps to this section** box appears. Drag and drop apps into this section based on your needs.

<div align="left"><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Unknown image (212)" alt=""></div>

### Read Notifications

You can read all notifications by clicking the icon. When there is a new message, the notifications list appears automatically as soon as you access your home page. You can reopen the list at any time to review previous messages. Notifications appear in the order they were received.

<div align="left"><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Unknown image (214)" alt=""></div>

### Benefits of using Okta

You can:

* “Stop memorizing passwords” because it offers one credential for all your apps.
* “Stop bookmarking website URLs” because it gives you access to all apps in one place.
* “Stop guessing if you have the right apps” because it auto-assigns the apps you need.
* “Start working from any device” because you can access apps from any internet-connected device.
