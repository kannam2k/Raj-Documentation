# How-to Videos

### 🎥 How-To Video Documentation Experience

In addition to written documentation, I contributed to developing **how-to videos** to support complex Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) processes. Addressing essential topics such as **Promotion Requests, Change Requests, and Change Notices** required a different documentation strategy—one that went beyond static text.

My role involved analyzing these intricate workflows, then designing interactive video tutorials that walked users through each step with clarity. By combining screen recordings, voice narration, and guided highlights, I transformed technical demonstrations into accessible learning resources. These videos not only explained the mechanics of PLM tasks but also contextualized their importance, helping teams understand both the how and the why.

The value added was significant:

* **Improved Adoption** – Users could visually follow processes, reducing confusion and errors.
* **Efficiency Gains** – Developers and support teams spent less time explaining workflows manually.
* **Consistency** – Every user had access to standardized, repeatable training materials.
