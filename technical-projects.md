# Technical Projects

### ⚙️ Technical Expertise Beyond Technical Writing

Alongside my work as a technical writer, I’ve contributed directly to projects that required hands-on technical and managerial expertise. One notable example was a **real estate company project**, where I played a dual role in both **dashboard creation** and **project management**.

* **Dashboard Creation on Zoho Platform**\
  I designed and implemented dashboards that tracked key parameters such as property listings, sales performance, client interactions, and financial metrics. By leveraging Zoho’s reporting and automation features, I provided stakeholders with real-time visibility into operations, enabling data-driven decision-making and improved efficiency.
* **Project Management**\
  Acting as a project manager, I coordinated cross-functional teams, streamlined workflows, and ensured timely delivery of project milestones. My role involved bridging communication between technical teams and business stakeholders, aligning requirements with execution, and maintaining accountability across the project lifecycle.
* **Data Migration (MS Dynamics → NetSuite)**\
  I led the migration of critical business information from Microsoft Dynamics to NetSuite. This required careful planning, mapping data structures, validating accuracy, and minimizing downtime. The migration ensured continuity of operations while enabling the company to leverage NetSuite’s advanced ERP capabilities.
