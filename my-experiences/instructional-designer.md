# Instructional Designer

### 🎬 Instructional Design Contribution – BE101 Project

In the BE101 project, I worked as an **Instructional Designer**, contributing to the preparation of storyboards that translated client requirements into structured learning materials. The project focused on developing company policies, which required a careful balance between accuracy, clarity, and instructional flow.

My role involved collaborating closely with stakeholders to understand their objectives, then designing **storyboards** that mapped out the learning journey step by step. These storyboards served as the foundation for training modules, ensuring that complex policy details were presented in a way that was both engaging and easy to follow. I incorporated scenarios, examples, and guided activities to make the content relatable and actionable for employees.

***

### ✨ Value Added

* **Clarity**: Simplified complex policy language into digestible learning segments.
* **Structure**: Designed logical flows that supported comprehension and retention.
* **Engagement**: Added interactive elements and real-world examples to make policies meaningful.
* **Alignment**: Ensured the final training materials reflected both client requirements and instructional best practices.
