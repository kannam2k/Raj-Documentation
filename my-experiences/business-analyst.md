# Business Analyst

### 📑 Business Analyst & Technical Writing Contribution – e‑Signature Project

In the e‑Signature project, I worked at the intersection of business analysis and technical writing, ensuring that both the functional requirements and the user experience were clearly defined and communicated. My role began with gathering and analyzing stakeholder needs—understanding how promotion requests, approvals, and compliance requirements shaped the workflow. I translated these insights into structured documentation that guided both developers and end-users.

As a **Business Analyst**, I contributed by:

* Mapping out end-to-end signing workflows and identifying key decision points.
* Documenting business rules, compliance requirements, and integration needs.
* Collaborating with developers to refine use cases and ensure alignment with organizational goals.

As a **Technical Writer**, I added value by:

* Creating user guides and process documentation that explained how to initiate, track, and complete e‑Signature requests.
* Developing clear release notes to communicate new features and updates.
* Authoring standard operating procedures (SOPs) that standardized signing processes across teams.

***

### ✨ Value Added

My dual contribution ensured that the project was not only technically sound but also **usable and scalable**. By combining analytical insight with clear documentation, I helped bridge the gap between business requirements and technical implementation—making the e‑Signature solution easier to adopt, reducing errors, and improving compliance across the organization.
